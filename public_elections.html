Public Elections

The following elections are open polls of opinion. Anyone may cast a vote. There is little attempt to prevent people from voting more than once, so the results of these polls should not be considered to be valid measures of public opinion. They are offered merely as a demonstration of how CIVS works. You may create your own open elections by checking the appropriate checkbox in the election creation form. However, they will not appear on this list.
